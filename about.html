About Us

Who We Are

HR-consulting specialises in assisting small to medium sized businesses
manage their human resources function. Our goal is to provide expert
HR advice and support in the most flexible and value-driven way possible.


What sets us apart is our highly flexible and personalised approach to
understand the unique culture and goals of your business. We become
an effective and trusted advisor aligned with your strategy, objectives
and way of doing things.


We are a respected HR consulting business based in Sydney, with
expertise across many aspects of HR. Ultimately, we help you to
manage employment risk and get the best out of your people.


Best of all, our unique services can be accessed remotely or on-site.
We can also cater for different service level requirements at different
stages and times in your business. This gives you the optimum level of
flexibility to access the HR support you need – when you need it.
